a web that’s powered
by the people, for the people.

Like past iterations of the web, the BitWeb is a place where applications, media and data can be consumed. Unlike those past iterations, the BitWeb is powered on the computers of its users, rather than centralized providers. Users like you control their own data, maintain their own sovereignity and experience the privacy and security they deserve. Likewise, BitWeb is a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO), where its users direct future growth and initiatives, rather than centralized telecom providers.
